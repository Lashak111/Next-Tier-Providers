FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

Is your consulting service free?

Yes. We offer a free IT review. We will review your IT environment for no fee and no expectations. Also, we will review how your IT is working and will highlight potential problems, and we will make sure your IT support or solution is right for your requirements. It is a way for us to get to know each other and add value to your managed IT services.

how can I be protected after giving you access to my systems?

Your company is officially protected even before giving access - before providing services, we sign a non-disclosure agreement (NDA), the details of which will be agreed with both you and your company's lawyers.

For solutions, which brands do you offer or suggest?

We offer solutions-based brands after understanding your needs and getting familiar with your existing systems and requirements. Upon gathering information, we will discuss with you the most suitable solution from the nineteen top international brands represented by NTP in our country. After considering all the above-mentioned and available budgets, we will provide solution offers for particular brands.

Is solution integration service free?

Yes, our offers include free integration. Through our solution integration, customers expect plug-and-play solutions and the true value of the solutions we sell is fully realized only with the right devices, accessories, in the right place and when other hardware and software is integrated. In order to make sure that the solution will be implemented properly for the company's future proof of its ecosystem, it will be easy to use, scalable, adapted and adjusted as business requirements change, we always recommend using our free integration service.

Are you promising that I will not have any technical problems or downtime after considering all your recommendations?

No, nobody can ever promise that. In spite of the fact that we implement BCDR solutions for us, it is all about proactively maintaining your systems to reduce the number and severity of problems, then dealing with any problems that do arise quickly and efficiently. In a nutshell, it’s how you deal with problems that truly matters. Try us out!
