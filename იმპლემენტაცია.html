Commissioning

Our installation and commissioning services are performed in accordance with the approved plan and detailed design documents. This includes systems integration, integration of data, applications, APIs, and devices across your organization to provide high efficiency, productivity, and agility in all of your operations.

NTP also offers consulting services related to installation and commissioning (supervision). By agreement with the customer, we can provide the following services: supervision of installation and commissioning works, development of test methods, staff training, etc.

Upon completion of installation and commissioning, NTP can perform the following works for the transfer and acceptance of the system into full-time operation:

⇨ Preliminary testing in order to check the functionality of the system and its compliance with the customer's technical requirements;
⇨ Technical and methodological assistance in the customer's experimental operation of the system;
⇨ Acceptance testing of the system, analysis of the results of experimental operation and testing, and the elimination of deficiencies;

NTP can train the customer's employees to operate the system, and assist in the preparation of organizational and administrative documentation and technical measures for system commissioning. Furthermore, by providing training, we can demonstrate techniques that can be applied to managing your systems afterwards through automation of processes and tasks or even orchestration to manage multiple automated processes and create a dynamic workflow.

Our customers receive the following benefits:

⇨ NTP adheres to international standards in management and uses an automated project management system that allows us to implement projects on schedule so you get installation and commissioning in the shortest possible time.
⇨ Upon completion, the customer receives installed, configured and operating equipment.
⇨ Verification of the system's compliance with the customer's requirements.;
⇨ Completed formal procedures to put the system into operation.
⇨ Project documentation and training provided by NTP allow the customer's IT professionals to ensure the most efficient use of IT resources, facilitate further improvement of the system, and reduce operation costs.
