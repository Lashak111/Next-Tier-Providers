Implementation & Integration

In the course of implementing its projects, NTP uses a systematic approach, when the projected system is considered, taking into account the functional interoperability of its components. Such an approach provides for in-depth analysis of the system and contributes to the successful completion of projects due to better planning and more effective design work.

Nuts and Bolts

Whether hardware is provided by NTP or supplied by another company, We will work with you to build detailed network architecture diagrams. mapping server location(s), connections, protocols, failover options, security, and all options to consider when setting up your hardware ecosystem.

Peace of Mind

Commissioning

Installation and commissioning are performed in accordance with the approved plan and detailed design documentation, which includes systems integration including connection of data, applications, APIs, and devices across your organization to be more efficient, productive, and agile to provide high efficiency, productivity and agility in all works.

NTP also offers consulting services relating to installation and commissioning (supervision). By agreement with the customer, we can provide the following services: supervision of installation and commissioning works, development of test methods, staff training, etc.

Upon completion of installation and commissioning, NTP can perform the following works for the transfer and acceptance of the system into full-time operation:

⇨ Preliminary testing in order to check the functionality of the system and its compliance with the customer's technical requirements;
⇨ Technical and methodological assistance in the customer's experimental operation of the system;
⇨ Acceptance testing of the system, analysis of results of the results of experimental operation and testing, and the elimination of deficiencies;

NTP can train the customer's employees to operate the system, and assist in the preparation of organizational and administrative documentation and technical measures for system commissioning.

Our customers receive the following benefits:

⇨ Installation and commissioning in the shortest possible time; Upon completion, the customer receives installed, configured and operating equipment;
⇨ Verification of the system's compliance with the customer's requirements and quality control over work performed under the project;
⇨ Formal procedures to put the system into operation;

Project documentation developed by NTP allows the customer's IT professionals to ensure the most efficient use of IT resources, facilitate further improvement of the system, and reduce operation costs.
